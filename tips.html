Tips to Get Started

Every piece of advice you need to begin a diet.

  • 1. Eat a variety of nutrient-rich foods.
  • 2. Include plenty of fruits and vegetables in your meals.
  • 3. Choose whole grains over refined grains.
  • 4. Include lean protein sources in your diet.
  • 5. Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
  • 6. Control portion sizes to avoid overeating.
  • 7. Limit added sugars and salt in your meals.
  • 8. Plan your meals and snacks ahead of time.
  • 9. Listen to your body's hunger and fullness cues.
  • 10. Be mindful of emotional eating triggers.
